OUR PORTFOLIOS

DEVELOPMENT

The Development portfolio oversees the club's project teams, organizing technical workshops and collaborating with external stakeholders to support members' growth and deliver real-world impact.

TECHNOLOGY

The Technology portfolio maintains the club's websites and tech infrastructure while exploring and implementing new technologies to benefit club operations and members.

FINANCE

The Finance portfolio manages the club's budgets, reimbursements, and financial planning to ensure all WDS initiatives run smoothly and resources are allocated effectively.

INTERNALS

The Internals portfolio is all about building community within WDS. Through events like our mentorship program, poker socials, and house parties, we create fun, low-pressure ways for members to meet and connect.

MARKETING

The marketing portfolio is the first point of contact with club members, creating engaging posts, stories, and reels while handling media production and keeping members connected year round

CAREERS

The Careers portfolio supports members' professional growth through career events, resume workshops, and partnerships with companies and alumni to create internship and job opportunities.

EXTERNALS

The Externals portfolio plans and runs tech events and workshops, helping students learn relevant skills, meet others, and get involved, making tech more approachable for everyone.
