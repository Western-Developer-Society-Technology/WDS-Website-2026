Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WDS, events and getting in contact with us

WDS hosts a wide variety of events including socials, conferences, hackathons, and workshops. Some of our events include Poker Night, Spark, WITS, and our Mentorship Program.

Whether you're looking to network, compete, learn, or just have fun, there's something at WDS for everyone.

As a WDS member you will get priority access to all of our events along with discounted tickets for paid events.

You’ll also gain access to our exclusive Discord community, where you have opportunities to network with like minded students and take advantage of the resume reviews to assist with career search.

You can sign up to become a member through our Linktree on our Instagram.

No prior experience is needed! WDS is open to students of all backgrounds and technical skill levels.

Our goal is to equip members with the skills, knowledge, and connections to help them grow, whether you're just starting out or already have experience in tech.

SheHacks+ is designed as a space that centers women+ and gender-marginalized hackers. While the majority of hacker spots are reserved for these groups, allies of all genders are welcome to support as mentors, volunteers, and sponsors.

If you are unsure where you fit, reach out to our team and we’ll be happy to chat.
