SheHacks+ 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about applying, attending, and making the most of your SheHacks+ experience.

Hacker applications open in early November and close in mid-January. Exact dates will be announced on our social channels and mailing list.

We review applications on a rolling basis, so we highly recommend applying as early as you can.

We are committed to making SheHacks+ welcoming and accessible for every hacker. When you apply, you’ll be able to tell us about any accommodations you need.

Common accommodations include:

  • Dietary needs (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, allergies)
  • Accessible seating and mobility access throughout the venue
  • Quiet and low-sensory spaces for breaks and prayer
  • Pronoun stickers and inclusive name badges

SheHacks+ will be hosted in person at Western University in London, Ontario. The main hacking space, workshops, and sponsor booths will all be located within a short walk of each other.

Detailed room locations, maps, and transit/parking options will be shared with accepted hackers closer to the event.

No. SheHacks+ is completely free for accepted hackers. Your ticket includes access to the venue, meals, swag, mentorship, and all workshops.

You are responsible for your own travel to and from the event, but we do our best to help coordinate carpools and share travel discounts where possible.

You can absolutely apply and attend without a team. Many hackers arrive solo and form teams during our icebreakers and team formation activities on day one.

If you already have a team, you’ll be able to list your teammates on your application so we can keep you together.

SheHacks+ is designed as a space that centers women+ and gender-marginalized hackers. While the majority of hacker spots are reserved for these groups, allies of all genders are welcome to support as mentors, volunteers, and sponsors.

If you are unsure where you fit, reach out to our team and we’ll be happy to chat.

Yes, we welcome recent graduates who have completed their studies within the last 12 months at the time of the event.

When you apply, simply indicate that you are a recent graduate and share the institution and program you completed.

Yes, international students are welcome to apply. However, please note that we are not able to provide visas or travel funding.

If you are studying outside Canada, make sure you have the appropriate documentation to travel and attend the event in person before accepting your offer.
